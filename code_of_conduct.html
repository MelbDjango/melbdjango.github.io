MelbDjango's Code of Conduct

MelbDjango aims to provide a friendly environment for all attendees and speakers, to do this we need the help of all participants at our events.

MelbDjango is dedicated to providing a great experience for everyone. We will not tolerate harrasment or abuse of any kind by event participants.

Be kind to others. Behave professionally. Make new friends and engage in interesting conversations with people. Remember that harassment and sexist, racist, or exclusionary comments are not appropriate at MelbDjango events.

Be approriate to the situation. MelbDjango has a professional audience including people of many different backgrounds. Please keep this in mind when preparing talks and taking part in discussions during the event.

Talk to us. Let us know if you encounter harassment or otherwise feel uncomfortable at any time during our events.

Thanks! Thank you for helping make our events a welcoming, friendly experience for all.
